# Slack Trust Center — Main Page
# Source: https://slack.com/trust
# Fetched: 2026-03-08

## Core Commitment

Slack is "committed to the security and privacy of your data," with a Trust Center designed to connect users to privacy, security, and compliance programs.

## Key Trust Pillars

### AI Principles
Slack documents how data privacy and security serve as foundations for its artificial intelligence and machine learning initiatives.

### Privacy
The company provides policies, tools, and procedures intended to protect user data and help organizations meet their privacy obligations.

### Security
Enterprise-grade security is described as built into every aspect of user collaboration and work processes within the platform.

### Compliance
Slack maintains a compliance program addressing major regulations including GDPR and CCPA, offering guidance for organizations navigating their own compliance requirements.

### Data Management
The company commits to providing transparency regarding policies and tools affecting how users manage their data.

### Data Requests
A Data Request Policy and Transparency Report offer insights into how third-party and law enforcement data requests are handled.

## Additional Resources

The Trust Center directs users to supplementary materials covering international data transfers, FedRAMP authorization, and Data Processing Addendum updates through the Slack blog.
