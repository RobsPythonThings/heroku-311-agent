## Service Overview

Salesforce Services (Hyperforce) encompasses a broad range of cloud-based products including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, Lightning Platform, Marketing Cloud, and numerous specialized solutions. The service applies to core offerings and Salesforce.org LLC services focused on nonprofit and education sectors.

## Security Assessments

Documentation includes regular vulnerability and penetration testing reports, ransomware mitigation summaries, and DDoS risk mitigation overviews. Annual disaster recovery and business continuity testing validates operational resilience across all regions.
